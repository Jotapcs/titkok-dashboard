Privacy Policy

This app is a private dashboard used only by the app owner to view TikTok account and video performance metrics.

The app uses TikTok Login Kit to authenticate the owner's TikTok account and may access basic profile information, account statistics, and public video metrics such as views, likes, comments, and shares.

No personal data is sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising.

The app stores access tokens only locally on the owner's device for authentication purposes.
